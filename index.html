About Me

I am a dedicated biotechnology researcher with a Master's degree in Human Genomics from Panjab University. My expertise spans cell and molecular biology, with a particular focus on cancer biology, human genetics, and therapeutic innovation.

Currently serving as a Junior Scientist and Business Development Executive at AAL Biosciences Research Pvt Ltd, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge science and strategic business development. My work involves executing cell-based in vitro assays, molecular techniques, and data analysis to support R&D and safety studies across biotech, pharma, and cosmetic industries.

With hands-on experience from prestigious institutions including CSIR-IMTech, PGIMER, and Max Super Specialty Hospital, I bring a comprehensive understanding of both research excellence and clinical applications.
